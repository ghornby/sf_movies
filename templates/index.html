San Francisco Filming Locations

About:

Over the years many movies have filmed at various San Francisco locations and this website allows you to search through and find these movie locations. You can filter in one of two ways. First, you can enter in a movie title and find all locations associated with that title. Second, you can use the mouse to click on the map to specify a location, and then find all movie locations within a given radius of that location.

Data is provided by DataSF.

This website was developed by: Greg Hornby
